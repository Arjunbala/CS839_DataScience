Film comedy Meet The Fockers, sequel to Meet The Parents, has topped the <loc>US</loc> box office for a third week.

Martin Scorsese's Howard Hughes biopic The Aviator came third with $7.6m (£4m) and a total of $42.9m (£22.8m).
